Security Alert: Phishing Scam Warning

Dear customers,

It has come to our attention that some customers have received fraudulent emails and text messages claiming to be from our bank, requesting sensitive information such as account numbers and passwords.

Please be aware that our bank will never ask you to provide confidential information through email or text messages. If you receive any suspicious communications, do not click on any links or provide any personal details. Instead, report the incident to our customer support immediately.
